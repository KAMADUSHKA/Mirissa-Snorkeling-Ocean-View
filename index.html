    About Us

    Welcome to "Mirissa Snorkeling Ocean View" – Your Gateway to Ocean Wonders!

    Located along the stunning southern coast of Sri Lanka, we specialize in creating unforgettable marine experiences. Whether it’s snorkeling with graceful sea turtles, diving into vibrant reef ecosystems, or watching whales glide across the horizon, our tours are designed to bring you closer to the magic of the ocean.

    We pride ourselves on providing:

    Top-quality gear for comfort and safety

    Affordable prices without compromising quality

    Friendly, knowledgeable guides who love what they do

    Sustainable practices that respect and protect marine life

    Come explore the ocean with us — where every dive, snorkel, or tour is a story worth remembering.
