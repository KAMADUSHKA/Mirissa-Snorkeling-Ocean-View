Deep Sea Fishing Tours

Take your fishing adventure to the next level with our Deep Sea Fishing Tour in Mirissa. This 4 to 5-hour trip takes you far offshore into deeper waters where big game fish like tuna, mahi-mahi, barracuda, sailfish, and even marlin can be found. It’s the perfect experience for fishing enthusiasts who want a real ocean challenge.

  • ⭐ Free Delicious Food or Fruits
  • ⭐ Duration: 4h - 5h
  • ⭐ Fish varieties : tuna, mahi-mahi, barracuda, sailfish

Led by an experienced local captain and crew, you'll be equipped with professional fishing gear, trolling and bottom fishing equipment, bait, and safety essentials. Whether you're an experienced angler or trying deep sea fishing for the first time, our team will guide you through every step, from casting to reeling in your catch.

The tour departs early morning or late afternoon, depending on your preference and weather conditions. Cold water and light refreshments are included, and we can arrange to clean your catch after the trip. Get ready for an exciting and rewarding fishing experience in the rich waters off Mirissa’s coast.
