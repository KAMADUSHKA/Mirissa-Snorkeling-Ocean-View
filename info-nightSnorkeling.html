Night Snorkeling Tours

Experience the ocean like never before with our Night Snorkeling Tour in Mirissa. This unique 2-hour adventure takes place after sunset, offering a completely different perspective of the underwater world. Armed with underwater torches, you'll explore the reef under the stars and discover marine life that only comes out at night.

Our trained guides will lead you safely through the calm night waters, pointing out nocturnal creatures like lobsters, sleeping fish, octopuses, and glowing plankton. We provide all the necessary equipment, including high-quality snorkeling gear and waterproof lights, to ensure a thrilling and secure experience.

Night snorkeling is safe, exciting, and truly unforgettable. It's perfect for those looking to go beyond the ordinary and try something new in Mirissa. Tours are small and personalized, and post-snorkel facilities such as showers and changing rooms are available for your comfort.
